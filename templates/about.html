About ChainVault

Welcome to ChainVault — a next-generation blockchain-based, end-to-end encrypted chat and file sharing platform designed for privacy, security, and freedom.

Our Mission

ChainVault ensures that your conversations and file transfers are completely private and tamper-proof. Built on blockchain technology, our app leverages decentralized infrastructure and military-grade encryption to keep your data safe from prying eyes — no central authority, no compromises.

Our Roots: BIET Davangere

This project is proudly developed by students from Bapuji Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET), Davangere — an institution known for nurturing innovation, technical excellence, and forward-thinking engineers. With the vision of building a better digital future, we strive to blend blockchain technology with real-world applications.
